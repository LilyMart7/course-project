Services & Prices
Prices
Confused about the difference in pricing of various writing services? We understand! Most services charge a set price-per-page, for anything from a high school 5-paragraph essay to a Ph.D. dissertation. This should raise an immediate red flag to you. No honest and ethical service can do this without using second-rate writers and databases of pre-written papers.
At EssaysService.com, our pricing is uniquely designed to fit all of the factors involved in creating an original, professionally-written work at any academic level and with any deadline required. The tables that follow will provide you important information about the cost of the work you need. The price-per-page is based upon double-spaced, standard font, of approximately 300 words per page. You will note that prices-per-page vary depending upon the following:

Length of paper – the greater the length requirement, the less per page cost

Deadline – the price declines as the deadline time frame lengthens

Academic level – high school writing is less expensive than that of college and graduate levels

Considering that we use degreed professionals who write each work from scratch as it is ordered, our prices are extremely reasonable. We are not the cheapest service, but we do not want to be. We are, however, as reasonable as you will find for the quality we deliver. There are many products and pricing levels that will meet your needs and fit your budget, and this is why so many students return to EssaysService.com over and over again. No matter what your writing need, we have the writer, the quality, and the price for you! We have a team of American and British professional writers who are committed to academic integrity. They produce authentic, original essays, research papers, term papers, theses, and dissertations, in part or whole, and stand ready to assist you, no matter what the need.
